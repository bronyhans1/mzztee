Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1st April 2026

Information we collect

When you book an appointment or contact us, we collect your name, email address, and phone number, along with any details you choose to share in your message (such as preferred date or garment notes).

How we use your information

We use this information only to handle your booking, respond to your enquiry, and communicate with you about your appointment or order. We do not use it for unrelated marketing unless you have agreed to that separately.

Sharing and selling data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share details only when necessary to run our business (for example, with a courier you have asked us to use) or where the law requires us to.

Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure; if you have concerns, please contact us using the email below.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, email us at hello@mzzteeklothing.com.
